Common Questions

Will we lose any data?

No. We migrate every record from your FileMaker database. We also validate the migrated data against the original to ensure nothing is lost or corrupted.

How much does this cost?

We provide a free estimate after reviewing your FileMaker application. Once we've scoped the work, you get a fixed price for the entire migration — no hourly billing, no surprises.

Can we keep using FileMaker during the migration?

Yes. We build the new system alongside your existing one. Your team continues using FileMaker until the new app is fully tested and ready. We handle the final data sync at cutover.

What happens after launch?

We provide documentation and training for your team. We also offer optional ongoing support and maintenance if you want us to continue managing updates and improvements.

Why Ruby on Rails?

Rails is a proven framework used by companies like Shopify, GitHub, and Basecamp. It's ideal for business applications: fast to develop, easy to maintain, and backed by a massive ecosystem of libraries. Your FileMaker data model maps naturally to Rails' database layer.
